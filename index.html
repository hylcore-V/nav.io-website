Our Mission

Navio is a decentralized high-utility privacy platform, offering novel solutions for maintaining your financial privacy, making private blockchain applications widely accessible. Built on the self-developed blsCT protocol, facilitating our Proof-of-Private-Stake (PoPS) consensus mechanism, all transactions on the Navio blockchain are private-by-default.

Our mission is to provide people with the needed tools to uphold their financial autonomy against the growing surveillance apparatus, so we are focusing on facilitating anonymous payments and decentralized banking.

Privacy is crucial and a fundamental human right. We believe that everyone around the globe should be able to make private transactions in order to protect their personal freedom, financial data, personal security, and business privacy, to name the most important areas - if you feel the same way, join us on our journey!

NAV

NAVIO (Ticker: NAV) is the privacy-by-default cryptocurrency within the Navio ecosystem, facilitating the blsCT protocol to protect your information by hiding the sending and receiving address, as well as the amount of coins sent.

Private

Financial privacy and being able to transact freely is a fundamental human right, a prerequisite for a free society, as the ability to pursue other freedom rights are directly connected.

Decentralized

As part of the Cypherpunk Movement, Navio generally aims to create decentralized, privacy-enhanced solutions that promote the freedom of the individual.

Private Tokens

Private tokens are a special feature of the Navio ecosystem, as they allow anyone to mint their own private tokens, which are created on top of the blsCT protocol.

Private NFTs

Public blockchains have shown to leak NFT metadata at large scale (e.g. ownership information) for everyone to see on block explorers. With Navio's private NFTs, there's no longer cause for concern.

Private dApps

Our privacy-features can be bridged to other ecosystems. If you are a developer who wants to build privacy solutions on a blockchain that offers mandatory privacy for all transactions, you can build your dApps on Navio.

Our Ecosystem

Navio stands as a privacy-centric, high-utility blockchain ecosystem, utilizing our blsCT protocol alongside our unique and world's first Proof-of-Private-Stake (PoPS) consensus mechanism, to provide a variety of features and use cases.

Our aim is to empower developers to create private decentralized dApps on top of Navio, to give more people the opportunity to maintain their financial privacy.

Private payments

The privacy coin of the Navio blockchain (NAV) can be used for private payments, as the sender, receiver and transferred amount always remains hidden, while transactions are scalable, fast and cost-effective.

To be able to perform private payments is very important in order to maintain your financial privacy.

If you are performing payments via Bitcoin, or any other pseudonymous cryptocurrency, your transaction history can be traced and potentially tied to your real identity, thanks to more and more KYC measures mandated by centralized exchanges.

Interoperability & DeFi

We see it as our responsibility to make interoperability between Navio and ecosystems such as Ethereum, Avalanche, BSC and others possible, giving pseudonymous tokens our privacy shield.

Privacy in the cryptocurrency space should be available to anyone, no matter what blockchain or ecosystem is used.

Proof-of-Private-Stake (PoPS)

PoPS is a Proof-of-Private-Stake implementation compatible with confidential schemes, facilitating Pedersen Commitments to hide transaction amounts, built around a combination of range proofs and a set membership proof. Neither the staked amount nor the identity of the staker are revealed.

A major change for the staker is the coin locking feature. Once an amount of coins is locked, the staker is entitled to participate in the staking of the following epochs which represent sets of consecutive blocks of a predetermined length.

Coins which are locked can not be spent, and are exclusively acting as collateral for securing the network. After an epoch is completed, stakers can unlock their coins.

Decentralized & autonomous

Our Proof-of-Private-Stake (PoPS) consensus mechanism allows NAV stakers to participate in decision-making by voting in consultations and proposals, with equal voting weight and no entry barriers for submissions.

These measure enable Navio to be self-funded, self-guided and self-governed through consensus changes, ensuring longevity beyond our lifetimes.

Navio as a platform

With great privacy comes great responsibility, and so we're incredibly excited for Navio to also serve as a high-utility privacy platform that new and existing ecosystems can use to obtain privacy with the same privacy-preserving properties as Navio itself.

Private tokens

Private tokens are a unique feature of the Navio ecosystem that allows anyone to mint their own private tokens, which are created on top of our blsCT protocol.

Transactions with those tokens are as private as regular NAV transactions; nobody can see how many private tokens you hold, the amount you send or to whom, or where the funds were originally coming from.

Other blockchain ecosystems which are not anonymous but pseudonymous, such as Ethereum, Avalanche and others, could profit from facilitating the Navio ecosystem to give their tokens a privacy-shield.

Private NFTs

Private NFTs allow for private ownership, ensuring assets and transactions do not need to be exposed to everyone, in opposite to traditional NFTs, where ownership and all transactions are open for everyone to see. In other words, anyone who owns a traditional NFT can be openly identified.

Private NFTs solve this problem by protecting ownership visibility with private NFTs. The validation of fungibility happens without opening verifiability to everyone.

This means that the process of validation occurs without compromising private data, including transfers and proof of authenticity.

Private DEX

Based on the Navio on-chain trading protocol and its utilisation of Atomic Swaps and BLS signatures, allowing trade to happen freely and privately, we are currently building a DEX for Navio users to be able to trade private wrapped versions of a multitude of tokens.

Our vision is to enable anyone to participate in a truly decentralized ecosystem that maintains one's privacy at all times; the private token DEX will be a key component in achieving this vision.
